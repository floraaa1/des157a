Welcome to Maze Adventure

Please read the rule carefully!

  • Players & Turns: Two players take turns rolling a die to move through the maze.
  • Movement: Move according to the die roll but only into open rooms. If trapped by doors, you can trade dice points to move back.
  • Reroll Option: If unsatisfied, you can reroll but must accept the new result.
  • Special Rule: Rolling a 1 means you stay in place for that turn.
  • Mystery Rooms: Rooms with (?) contain surprise boxes with rewards or effects.
  • Winning condition: First player to reach the exit wins!
